Why is this
IMPORTANT

Oil spills damage marine ecosystems by introducing toxins, destroying habitats, and disrupting food chains. They also impact fisheries and tourism, pose health risks, and cause wildlife mortality. Long-term ecological recovery can take decades. Early detection and response using AIS and satellite data are crucial for minimizing damage. This system improves monitoring, supports regulatory compliance, and reduces cleanup costs. The project integrates advanced technology, contributing to marine conservation and offering valuable research opportunities.

An optimal solution for enhancing early oil spill detection and response, minimizing environmental damage and economic losses while promoting sustainable maritime practices.
